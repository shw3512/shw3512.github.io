Resources

  • Khan Academy : Incredible resource for written explanations, videos, and practice problems.
    • Algebra 1. For Math 111|112, mastery of these concepts is helpful.
    • Algebra 2. For Math 111|112, the modules on polynomials (arithmetic, factorization, division, and graphs), rational exponents and radicals, logarithms, transformations of functions, equations, and trigonometry are relevant.
    • Geometry. For Math 111|112, the modules on similarity, right triangles and trigonometry, and analytic geometry are relevant.
    • Precalculus. For Math 111|112, in addition to the topics already mentioned above, the modules on rational functions and limits and continuity are relevant.
  • LaTeX : Selected LaTeX resources are listed below.
    • Detexify : Online app that lets you hand-draw LaTeX symbols, and the app returns the LaTeX code.
    • LaTeX : Typesetting software.
    • LyX : Graphical user interface overlay for LaTeX.
    • Overleaf : Online collaborative LaTeX editor.
