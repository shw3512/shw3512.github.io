    • Complex numbers. For Math 211, we'll need all the lessons in this module, except (5) Distance and midpoint. Note: For Math 211, we may not encounter division of complex numbers, but once we know how to multiply complex numbers, how to compute complex conjugates, and how to write complex numbers in polar form, we can pick up division with just a little extra effort.
    • Derivatives (ordinary). For Math 211, the lessons after Quiz 1, focusing on how to compute various ordinary derivatives, are all important.
    • Derivatives (partial). For Math 211, we'll need the following lessons: (1) Partial derivatives; (5) Multivariable chain rule (just to compute the first-order partial derivatives of functions with multiple inputs); (7) Partial derivatives of vector-valued functions. Lesson (13) Jacobian will also be of interest to us when we study linearization.
    • Differential equations. For Math 211, we'll be expected to be able to do all the math in this module, so it serves as a great source for another perspective and for practice!
