Because $f$ is a polynomial, its gradient is defined everywhere; hence all critical points of $f$ on the interior of $D$ satisfy
\begin{align*}
\fontVector{0}
=
\gradient f
=
\left(2 x - 2,2 y + 2\right)
&
&\Leftrightarrow
&
(x,y)
&=
(1,-1).
\end{align*}
