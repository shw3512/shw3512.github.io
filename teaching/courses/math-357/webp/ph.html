Practice homework: Suggested exercises

All exercises listed below come from Dummit & Foote, 3e, unless noted otherwise.

Please do not feel bound by the suggestions here. There are many excellent candidates for practice homework, including

  • Exercises in Dummit & Foote and other textbooks
  • Worked-out examples and proofs in Dummit & Foote and other textbooks
  • Ideas arising in class or in discussion with your colleagues

My goal with the practice homework is to help you develop a habit of regularly (!) and deliberately working on what you need to grow as a mathematician and to be prepared for the assessments in this course.
